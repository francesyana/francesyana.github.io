Case Study Part 2

This section presents Case Study Part 2: Creating Users.

Steps

  1. 2. Add domain users (distribute IT student names to all dept/section/area)
  2. I. 4 Academic departments
  3. 1. Basic Art and Sciences Department
  4. a. BTVTE Electrical
  5. b. BTVTE Electronics
  6. c. BTVTE Computer Hardware
  7. d. BTVTE Computer Software
  8. 2. Electrical and Allied Department
  9. a. information technology
  10. b. electrical engineering
  11. c. electronics engineering
  12. d. instrumentation and control technology
  13. e. BET Mechatronics
  14. 3. Mechanical and Allied Department
  15. a. mechanical engineering technology
  16. b. rac engineering technology
  17. c. non destructive engineering technology
  18. d. electromechanical engineering technology
  19. e. automotive engineering technology
  20. f. BET ElectroMechanical
  21. 4. Civil and Allied Department
  22. a. civil engineering
  23. b. civil engineering technology
  24. c. environmental science
  25. d. chemical engineering technology
  1. II. administrative department consists of the following:
  2. 1. registrar's office
  3. 2. director's office
  4. 3. research and extension
  5. 4. human resource and management office
  6. 5. finance office
  7. 6. supply and procurement
  8. 7. records office
  9. 8. library services
  1. III. create domain users for each department/office and technology section. use their real names when creating the domain account
  2. IV. create an organizational unit for each technology section or office (i.e. computer engineering, electronics, registrar, research etc.)
  3. V. all users can only log on to workstations (xp class a networl, 7 class b network) that were assigned to their respective departments/offices
  4. - users → properties → Computer Restrictions → select which computer.
  5. VI. all users should change their passwords every 30 days
  6. - Go to Grou[p Policy Management → create a GPO for the org unit Academic & Administrative → For academic: Academic Password , For Administrative: Administrative Password.
  7. - Click Edit → Computer Config → Software Settings → Security Settings → Account Policies → Password Policies → Maximum Password Age → Properties → 30 days → apply → ok
  8. VII. academic users can log on from 7:30 am - 8:45am while administrative staff users can log on from 7:00am - 7:00pm
  9. - Select Users in each Department in academic → Properties → Account → Check log on hours and select time range then log on permitted.
