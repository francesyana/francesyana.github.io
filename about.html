About

Lorraine Frances De Sagun

BSIT-NS-2A-T

This website has been developed as the final project for the Operating Systems course under the guidance of Dr. Rico Santos. Its primary objective is to provide a structured and accessible resource on the installation procedure, setup, configuration, and management of the Windows Operating System.

The site includes comprehensive instructional materials, such as step-by-step video demonstrations hosted on YouTube, as well as a series of laboratory activities designed to strengthen practical skills in operating system management.
